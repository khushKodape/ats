Problems We Solve

Real issues faced by farmers in Bhandara, Vidarbha — and our technology-driven solutions.

🚁
Pesticide Spraying
⚠️ Problem

Farmers spray pesticides manually, causing serious infections and health issues on their face and body due to direct chemical exposure.

✅ Our Solution

🚁 Drone Spraying Technology — Automated drones spray pesticides precisely across fields with zero human exposure, keeping farmers safe and healthy.

🌿
Plant Health Monitoring
⚠️ Problem

Farmers cannot easily tell if their crops are diseased or malnourished. They rely on guesswork which leads to major crop loss.

✅ Our Solution

💻 Computerized Plant Health Checking — AI-powered cameras scan crops and automatically detect disease or nutritional deficiencies in real time.

💧
Smart Water Supply
⚠️ Problem

Water is either wasted by over-watering or crops die from under-watering. Farmers have no way to measure the exact moisture level needed.

✅ Our Solution

🌡️ Moisture Sensor System — Smart soil sensors check moisture levels in real-time and automatically supply the exact amount of water crops need.

🌾
Correct Fertilizer Use
⚠️ Problem

Farmers apply the wrong fertilizer at wrong times in wrong quantities — damaging soil quality and wasting money on ineffective treatment.

✅ Our Solution

🤖 Computerized Vision System — AI analyzes soil and crop conditions to recommend and apply the correct fertilizer at the correct time in the correct amount.

🔐
Farming Field Security
⚠️ Problem

Farmer fields are unprotected at night, allowing animals, thieves and trespassers to destroy or steal crops, causing huge financial losses.

✅ Our Solution

⚡ Electric Conductivity Fence — A smart electricity conductivity system installed around the entire field creates a secure perimeter protecting crops 24/7.

Our Journey

From an idea in a small room in Bhandara to building real technology for Vidarbha's farmers.

2023

The Beginning

Atharv and Siddharth noticed farmers in their village getting sick from pesticide spraying. They started researching drone technology and AI solutions.

Early 2024

First Prototype

Built the first drone sprayer prototype using locally sourced parts. Tested on a 2-acre paddy field in Mohadi with farmer Ramesh Patil.

Mid 2024

5 Solutions Ready

Expanded from drone spraying to plant health AI, smart irrigation, fertilizer AI, and field security. ATS now offers a complete farm tech package.

2024 - Now

Serving Vidarbha

Actively working with farmers across Bhandara, Nagpur, Gondia, and Chandrapur districts. Every week, more farmers join the ATS family.
